Geography of Global Energy Usage

Climate change is a crisis facing our planet and everyone on it. Through uses of traditional, fossil fuel based energy sources, excessive amounts of carbon dioxide have been released into the atmosphere over the span of decades which is quickly beginning to unnaturally warm the planet. Warming temperatures then cause a multitude of problems such as severe weather, flooding and species extinction. The following visualization explores the regions of the world that have the most total energy usage, regardless of energy type.


The type of energy used by any given nation or region is an extremely important consideration in the climate change discussion. Traditional energy sources such as oil and coal produce the most pollutants. Throughout the years, research has been dedicated to creating cleaner, renewable energy sources. As the air warms with increased pollutants, the ocean in turn also warms. Warmer oceans mean increased moisture in the air which then causes increased severe weather systems.
